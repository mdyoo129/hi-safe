About Us

Why Choose HiSafe?

HiSafe is a mental health application with various features that can meet the needs of its users with a simple, simple and complete appearance.

One of the features is Articles, which are useful for users so they can get information about mental health such as tips, facts and news that is currently hot and of course related to mental health. Next is the Mood Tracker feature, which is useful for saving moods and seeing mood developments over certain time intervals. Apart from that, HiSafe also provides a Playlist feature, which is useful for relaxing the mind with calm music. By using this application regularly and following useful tips from counselors, users can feel changes in themselves for the better.
